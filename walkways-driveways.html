What we install

Engineered for vehicle loads & foot traffic alike.

Driveways carry a different load than patios — they need a deeper, stronger base. We engineer accordingly, every time.

  • Paver driveways — full driveway replacements & aprons
  • Paver walkways — front entries, side paths, garden paths
  • Entry courts & circular drives — luxury home entries
  • Flagstone & bluestone walks — natural, classic finishes
  • Permeable paver systems — drainage-friendly options
  • Step systems & landings — integrated with walkways

Driveway base spec

For driveways we increase the base depth and use heavier-gauge edge restraints. Vehicles flex pavers far more than feet do — undersized bases fail.

  • 10–12" of compacted CA-6 limestone
  • Geotextile fabric in soft soil conditions
  • Steel or concrete edge restraints
  • Proper grade for water shed
