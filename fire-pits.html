Fire Pits Built for
Long Illinois Evenings.

A great fire pit turns a backyard into a destination. We design and build custom fire features — wood-burning or gas, freestanding or fully integrated into your patio and seating walls — every one of them code-compliant and built to last.

Design Your Fire Pit Call 773-501-8699
Styles

From rustic to architectural.

We work with you on style, fuel type, and integration with the rest of your outdoor space — then handle the gas line coordination, code compliance, and finish work.

  • Stacked stone fire pits — natural, rustic look
  • Paver block fire pits — clean, modern lines
  • Custom masonry fire pits — full architectural designs
  • Gas-fueled fire features — flip-of-a-switch convenience
  • Wood-burning pits — traditional, full-flame experience
  • Integrated seat walls — built-in seating around your fire

Done the right way

  • Proper clearance from structures & combustibles
  • Fire-rated firebrick interior on wood-burning units
  • Steel inserts for thermal protection
  • Coordination with licensed gas plumbers when needed
  • Drainage so your pit doesn't fill with rainwater
