About G&D.

From landscaping origins to a full-service exterior contractor — built on quality materials, superior workmanship, and the same crews that have earned long-term client trust.

G&D was founded as a landscaping company built on the belief that exceptional results begin with both quality materials and superior workmanship. What started as a landscape operation steadily expanded as clients placed their trust in our team and requested additional exterior improvement services. Today, our expertise extends into hardscaping, including patios, retaining walls, fire pits, walkways, decks, and other custom outdoor living spaces designed to enhance residential and commercial properties.

As our company evolved, so did our commitment to protecting and maintaining the properties we serve year-round. This led to the development of our commercial snow removal division, which provides dependable winter maintenance services exclusively for commercial properties. Our snow operations are supported by the same experienced crews, equipment, and standards of professionalism that clients rely on throughout the construction season — ensuring consistency, reliability, and accountability in every service we provide.

In addition, G&D proudly operates G&D Materials, our Lake County materials yard, supplying high-quality bulk landscape products including mulch, topsoil, gravel, sand, and stone. We serve landscape contractors, commercial accounts, homeowners, and support our own construction and landscape projects with dependable materials and delivery solutions.

Our Mission

At G&D, our mission remains the same: deliver quality workmanship, dependable service, and long-term value through every season.
