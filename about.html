The Crew Behind
Every G&D Project.

G&D Construction & Snow Removal is built around one straightforward idea: do the work right, show up when promised, and keep showing up year after year. We're a family-owned business in Lake County, IL, and our customers tend to stay with us for a long time.

Our Story

Two seasons of work,
one team.

G&D started as a landscaping operation rooted in the belief that the materials and the workmanship both have to be right. From there we grew into hardscaping — patios, walls, fire pits, decks — because clients kept asking us to do more.

Snow removal came naturally. Once you've built something for somebody, you don't want to leave their property unattended in winter. Today our snow contracts run from the same crew that did the summer construction work — same people, same trucks, same standards.

We also operate G&D Materials, our materials yard in Lake County that supplies bulk mulch, topsoil, gravel, sand, and stone — to other landscapers, to homeowners, and yes, to our own job sites.

What you get with G&D

  • Owner on-site. Dalyne is on every job — not subcontracted out.
  • One contractor, multiple seasons. Build in summer, plowed in winter.
  • Materials direct. No middlemen — products from our own yard.
  • Real local crew. Family-owned, Lake County based, no national franchise overhead.
  • Documentation. Photos, written quotes, contracts that are clear.
